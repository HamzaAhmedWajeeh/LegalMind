AMENDMENT NO. 2 TO MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Amendment No. 2 ("Amendment") to the Master Services Agreement dated
June 15, 2023 (the "Agreement") is entered into as of January 1, 2024
("Amendment Effective Date") by and between:

Apex Corporation ("Client")
and
Vendor Technology Ltd. ("Vendor")

(each a "Party" and collectively the "Parties").


RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties entered into the Agreement on June 15, 2023;

WHEREAS, Amendment No. 1 was executed on September 1, 2023, expanding the
scope of Services to include cloud infrastructure management;

WHEREAS, the Parties now wish to amend certain provisions of the Agreement
to reflect the expanded commercial relationship and to update the liability
framework accordingly;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants herein, the Parties
agree as follows:


1. AMENDMENT TO SECTION 8.1 — INDEMNIFICATION LIABILITY CAP

Section 8.1 of the Agreement is hereby amended by deleting the final sentence
in its entirety and replacing it with the following:

"The aggregate liability of Vendor under this Section 8.1 shall not exceed
the total fees paid by Apex Corp to Vendor in the twenty-four (24) months
immediately preceding the claim giving rise to liability, effective January 1,
2024. For claims arising prior to January 1, 2024, the original twelve (12)
month cap shall continue to apply."

Rationale: The Parties acknowledge that, following the expansion of Services
under Amendment No. 1, the increased scope and criticality of Vendor's
obligations to Client warrant an increase in the indemnification liability cap.


2. AMENDMENT TO SECTION 3.2 — PAYMENT TERMS

Section 3.2 of the Agreement is hereby amended to read in its entirety as
follows:

"Vendor shall submit invoices to Client on the first business day of each
calendar month for Services performed in the preceding month. Payment is due
within forty-five (45) days of the invoice date ("Payment Due Date"). Client
may dispute any invoice in good faith by providing written notice to Vendor
within fifteen (15) days of receipt, specifying in reasonable detail the basis
for the dispute. Undisputed amounts shall remain due and payable."

The prior thirty (30) day payment term is superseded by this Amendment.


3. NEW SECTION 8.5 — CYBER INCIDENT LIABILITY

The following new Section 8.5 is hereby added to the Agreement:

"8.5 Cyber Incident Liability. Notwithstanding Section 8.3, in the event of
a data breach directly caused by Vendor's failure to maintain the security
standards set forth in Section 6.3, Vendor's liability for direct damages
arising from such breach shall not be subject to the exclusion of consequential
damages set forth in Section 8.3, and shall be subject to a separate aggregate
cap of five million US dollars (USD $5,000,000) per incident. This Section 8.5
creates a carve-out to Section 8.3 solely with respect to data breach liability
and does not otherwise expand Vendor's liability under this Agreement."


4. AMENDMENT TO SECTION 9.1 — TERM

Section 9.1 is hereby amended to extend the Initial Term by an additional
two (2) years. The Agreement shall now expire on June 15, 2028, unless
earlier terminated in accordance with its terms.


5. NEW SECTION 13 — AUDIT RIGHTS

The following new Section 13 is hereby added to the Agreement:

"13.1 Audit Rights. Client shall have the right, upon thirty (30) days'
prior written notice and no more than once per calendar year, to audit
Vendor's books, records, and systems relating to the Services to verify
compliance with this Agreement, including data protection obligations under
Section 6.

13.2 Audit Costs. The cost of any such audit shall be borne by Client,
unless the audit reveals a material discrepancy or non-compliance, in which
case Vendor shall bear the reasonable costs of the audit.

13.3 Cooperation. Vendor shall cooperate fully with any such audit and shall
provide Client and its auditors with reasonable access to relevant facilities,
systems, personnel, and documentation."


6. RATIFICATION

All other terms and conditions of the Agreement remain unchanged and in full
force and effect. In the event of any conflict between this Amendment and the
Agreement, the terms of this Amendment shall control.


7. COUNTERPARTS AND EXECUTION

This Amendment may be executed in counterparts and by electronic signature,
each of which shall be deemed an original and all of which together shall
constitute one and the same instrument.
